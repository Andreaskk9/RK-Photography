About Me

I have never been the one to sit in a room for too long. There's something in me that calms me when I'm out and about. Growing up in Kampala (Uganda) always kept me on my toes, I never knew what adventure was out there waiting.

Raian Konstantinidis Photography is a reflection of my journey in the world of photography. It all started with my cousins and I making a YouTube video. Not long after, a friend lent me his camera and I soon found myself shooting for a magazine. I went on to work for a photography and marketing group before finally setting up my own studio.

My passion for capturing beautiful and meaningful moments has always pushed me towards new and exciting projects. I strive to provide the best possible service to my clients and always look to capture the perfect shot.
